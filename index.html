Petster Exotic

Full stack MERN app for those who own exotic pets and are looking for a pet sitter, or those looking to find exotic pet-sitting opportunities.

Users can register an account by role, update account information, save favorite profiles, search by location or description, contact other users by email or video call, edit a calendar and use Paypal to tip pet sitters.

Uses the Material-UI and Fullcalendar.io frameworks, integrates the Mapbox, Sendgrid, Jitsi and Cloudinary APIs. On the back end, uses JWT and Passport for authentication and authorization, and bcrypt for password hashing. Includes a number of npm packages including Faker, sweetalert, moment and more!

Wyncode final project; winner of the Pitch Day contest. Visit the deployed site here!
